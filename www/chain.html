Chain

Chain drives transmit torque between sprockets, commonly used in drivetrains, elevators, and arms. ANSI #25 and #35 roller chains are standard for robotics, balancing strength, weight, and flexibility. Teams favor chain for its reliability, adjustability, and ease of field repairs.
