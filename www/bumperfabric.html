Bumper Fabric

Bumper fabric protects your robot and displays team colors on the field. Most teams use heavy-duty Cordura or ballistic nylon for durability through impact and repeated use. Good fabric choices make bumpers easier to build, maintain, and keep looking professional throughout the season.
