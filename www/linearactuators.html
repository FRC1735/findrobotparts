Linear Actuators

Linear actuators translate rotary motion into straight-line movement, ideal for lifts, clamps, and adjusting mechanisms. They provide controlled extension and retraction using screw or motor drive systems. Actuators simplify mechanical design where pneumatics aren’t practical or precise enough.
