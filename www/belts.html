Belts

Belts transfer rotational power quietly and efficiently between pulleys. Timing belts are especially popular for drivetrains, elevators, and indexing systems due to their smooth motion and easy tensioning. They’re lighter than chains, require less maintenance, and allow compact designs in tight spaces.
