Description

A plate bracket used for joining two pieces of 2×1 or 1×1 tubing at a 90 degree angle.  These brackets are commonly used on West Coast-style drivetrains.  Here are some feature highlights:

  • .080in thick for a balance between strength and weight savings.
  • .196in holes for use with #10 fasteners or 3/16im rivets.
  • Sold in packs of 2.
  • Laser-cut from 5052 Aluminum.
  • Weighs approximately .044 pounds.
